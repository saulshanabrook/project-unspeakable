The Deep State

  • Posted on March 12, 2014
  • by Leslie Sullivan Sachs
  • in News

“The Deep State, based on the twin pillars of national security imperative and corporate hegemony, has until recently seemed unshakable and the latest events may only be a temporary perturbation in its trajectory. But history has a way of toppling the altars of the mighty.”

Project Unspeakable shakes the twin pillars by unearthing the truth about the history of the national security / corporate Deep State.

Read Anatomy of the Deep State: Beneath Veneer of Democracy, The Permanent Ruling Class, by  Mike Lofgren author of The Party’s Over and “a congressional staff member for 28 years specializing in national security.”
