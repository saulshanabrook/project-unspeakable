Privacy Policy for ProjectUnspeakable.com<\/strong><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

This privacy policy has been compiled to better serve those who are concerned with how their ‘Personally identifiable information’ (PII) is being used online. Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information in accordance with our website.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

When do we collect information?<\/strong><\/p>\n

\u00a0<\/strong><\/p>\n

We collect information from you when you submit forms such as contact or event forms, subscribe to a newsletter, or comment on blog posts on our site.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

What personal information do we collect from the people that visit our website?<\/strong><\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

When submitting forms, or commenting on the blog, you are asked to enter your name, email address, and other details needed to help us fulfill your request.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

How do we use your information?<\/strong><\/p>\n

\u00a0<\/strong><\/p>\n

We may use the information we collect from you when you comment or submit a form, or use certain other site features in the following ways:<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

    \n
  • To fulfill your request.<\/li>\n
  • To send periodic emails regarding Project Unspeakable news and events.<\/li>\n
  • To improve our website in order to better serve you.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

     <\/p>\n

    Do we use ‘cookies’?<\/strong><\/p>\n

    \u00a0<\/strong><\/p>\n

    Yes. Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the site’s systems to recognize your browser and remember certain information.<\/p>\n

     <\/p>\n

    We use cookies to:<\/strong><\/p>\n

    \u00a0<\/strong><\/p>\n

      \n
    • Allow our website to recognize if you have previously entered information on one of our forms.<\/li>\n
    • Compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interactions in order to offer better site experiences and to better understand our audience.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

       <\/p>\n

      You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies. You do this through your browser (like Internet Explorer) settings. Each browser is a little different, so look at your browser’s Help menu to learn the correct way to modify your cookies.<\/p>\n

       <\/p>\n

      If you disable cookies, some features will be disabled that make your site experience more efficient, and some of our services may not function properly.<\/p>\n

       <\/p>\n

      How does our site handle do not track signals?<\/strong><\/p>\n

       <\/p>\n

      We honor do not track signals and do not track or plant cookies when a Do Not Track (DNT) browser mechanism is in place. It’s also important to note that we do not allow third party behavioral tracking.<\/p>\n

      \u00a0<\/strong><\/p>\n

      Third Party Disclosure<\/strong><\/p>\n

      \u00a0<\/strong><\/p>\n

      We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information<\/em> unless we provide you with advance notice. This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. Non-personally identifiable (anonymous) visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing or other uses. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others’ rights, property, or safety.<\/p>\n

       <\/p>\n

      ProjectUnspeakable.com uses the following third party services:<\/p>\n

       <\/p>\n

        \n
      • We use MailChimp to help us manage our email lists and newsletters. The privacy policy for MailChimp may be viewed at mailchimp.com\/legal\/privacy\/<\/a><\/li>\n
      • Google analytics are used on this site to help us evaluate visitors’ use of the website and to compile statistical reports on website activity. The tool uses cookies to collect standard Internet log information in an anonymous form. Google\u2019s privacy policy can be found at google.com\/intl\/en\/policies\/privacy\/<\/a>. We will never (and will not allow any third party to) use the statistical analytics tool to track or to collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) of visitors to our site.<\/li>\n
      • Donations to Project Unspeakable are processed either by PayPal, or by Indiegogo in the case of our NY debut crowdfunding campaign.<\/em><\/strong> These services have their own privacy statements which can be viewed by clicking on the corresponding links within each respective website. Since we do not have control over the policies or practices of participating merchants and other third parties, we are not responsible for the privacy practices or contents of those sites. We recommend you review their policies before you provide any personal information or complete any transaction with them. At no time does the website at projectunspeakable.com handle your financial information, and none of your financial details are shared with Project Unspeakable or its staff.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

         <\/p>\n

        Third party links<\/strong><\/p>\n

         <\/p>\n

        Occasionally, at our discretion, we may link to third party sites or services on our website. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.<\/p>\n

         <\/p>\n

        In order to be in line with Fair Information Practices:<\/strong><\/p>\n

        \u00a0<\/strong><\/p>\n

        Should a data breach occur exposing Personally Identifiable Information we will notify users via email within 7 business days.<\/p>\n

         <\/p>\n

        California Online Privacy Protection Act<\/strong><\/p>\n

         <\/p>\n

        In accordance with CalOPPA we agree to the following:<\/p>\n

          \n
        • Users can visit our site anonymously.<\/li>\n
        • Our Privacy Policy link includes the word ‘Privacy’, and can be easily be found on the home page<\/li>\n
        • Users will be notified of any privacy policy changes on our privacy policy page.<\/li>\n
        • Users are able to change their personal information by emailing us.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

           <\/p>\n

          COPPA (Children Online Privacy Protection Act)<\/strong><\/p>\n

           <\/p>\n

          We do not specifically market to children under 13, and do not knowingly collect any information from them.<\/p>\n

           <\/p>\n

          CAN-SPAM Act<\/strong><\/p>\n

          \u00a0<\/strong><\/p>\n

          The CAN-SPAM Act is a US Federal law that sets the rules for commercial email, establishes requirements for commercial messages, gives recipients the right to have emails stopped from being sent to them, and spells out penalties for violations.<\/p>\n

           <\/p>\n

          To be accordance with CAN-SPAM we agree to the following:<\/strong><\/p>\n

            \n
          • To NOT use false, or misleading subjects or email addresses.<\/li>\n
          • Identify the message as marketing in some reasonable way.<\/li>\n
          • Include the physical address of our business headquarters.<\/li>\n
          • Monitor third party email marketing services for compliance<\/li>\n
          • Honor opt-out\/unsubscribe requests quickly.<\/li>\n
          • Allow users to unsubscribe by using a link at the bottom of each email.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

             <\/p>\n

            If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, you can follow the instructions at the bottom of each email.<\/strong><\/p>\n

            \u00a0<\/strong><\/p>\n

            If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy you may contact us using the information below:<\/p>\n
