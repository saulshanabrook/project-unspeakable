Selected Script Bibliography

The resources below are selected from the Full Script Bibliography and were used in preparing the script.

Books

The Assassinations:  Probe Magazine on JFK, MLK, RFK and Malcolm X, Eds. James DiEugenio and Lisa Pease (Port Townsend, WA:  Feral House, 2003).

DiEugenio, James.  Destiny Betrayed:  JFK, Cuba and the Garrison Case (New York:  Skyhorse Publishing, 2012).

Douglass, James.  JFK and the Unspeakable:  Why He Died and Why It Matters (New York:  Touchstone, 2008).

Fonzi, Gaeton.  The Last Investigation:  What Insiders Know About the Assassination of JFK (New York:  Skyhorse Publishing, 2013).

King, Martin Luther and Coretta Scott King.  The Words of Martin Luther King, Jr. (New York:  Newmarket Press, 1987).

Klaber, William and Philip H. Melanson.  Shadow Play:  The Murder of Robert F. Kennedy, the Trial of Sirhan Sirhan, and the Failure of American Justice (New York:  St. Martin’s Press, 1997).

Merton, Thomas.  Raids on the Unspeakable (New York:  New Directions Publishing Corporation, 1966).

Pepper, William F.  An Act of State:  The Execution of Martin Luther King (London:  Verso, 2003).

—–, Orders to Kill:  The Truth Behind the Murder of Martin Luther King (New York:  Carroll & Graf Publishers, Inc., 1995).

Ratcliffe, David.  Understanding Special Operations (And Their Impact on the Vietnam War Era) (Santa Cruz:  rat haus reality press, 1999). http://ratical.org/ratville/JFK/USO/

Schotz, E. Martin.  History Will Not Absolve Us:  Orwellian Control, Public Denial, and the Murder of President Kennedy (Brookline, MA:  Kurtz, Ulmer, & DeLucia Book Publishers, 1996). http://ratical.org/ratville/JFK/HWNAU/

Strickland, William with Cheryll Y. Greene ed., Malcolm X:  Make It Plain (New York:  Viking, 1994).

Sullivan, William C. with Bill Brown.  The Bureau:  My Thirty Years in Hoover’s FBI (New York:  W.W. Norton & Company, 1979).

Talbot, David.  Brothers:  The Hidden History of the Kennedy Years (New York:  Free Press, 2007).

Twomey, Gerald ed., Thomas Merton:  Prophet in the Belly of a Paradox (New York:  Paulist Press, 1978).

X, Malcolm with Alex Haley.  The Autobiography of Malcolm X (New York:  Ballantine Books, 1965).

X, Malcolm.  Malcolm X Speaks 1st ed, Ed. George Breitman (New York:  Pathfinder Press, 1965).

Websites, Online Articles

http://www.ratical.org/ratville/JFK/:  Texts of Jim Douglass, Vincent Salandra, E. Martin Schotz, Fletcher Prouty, the JFK and Martin Luther King, Jr. assassinations conspiracy files, and many other resources related to the assassinations and covert operations.

http://www2.gwu.edu/~nsarchiv/:  National Security Archive interviews and documents (George Washington University)

http://www.maryferrell.org:  Church Committee, House Select Committee on Assassinations reports, other documents

http://www.archives.gov/research/jfk/: House Select Committee on Assassinations, Warren Commission reports

http://mlk-kpp01.stanford.edu/index.php/resources/article/annotated_letter_from_birmingham/:  Martin Luther King’s Letter from a Birmingham Jail

http://mlk-kpp01.stanford.edu/index.php/encyclopedia/documentsentry/doc_beyond_vietnam/:  Martin Luther King’s “Beyond Vietnam” speech

http://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Speech_on_the_Assassination_of_Martin_Luther_King,_Jr.:  Robert F. Kennedy’s  “Speech on the Assassination of Martin Luther King, Jr.,” April 4, 1968.

http://www.rfksafilm.org/html/speeches/unicape.php: Robert F. Kennedy’s “Day of Affirmation” speech, Cape Town, South Africa, June 6, 1966.

http://research.archives.gov/description/194034:  Robert F. Kennedy’s announcement as candidate for president

Woodworth, Elizabeth.  “The Climate Bomb:  Failures to Confront the Unspeakable and The Way Ahead, Part II.”  Global Research.  April 7, 2013.  http://www.globalreserach.ca/the-climate-bomb-failures-to-confront-the unspeakable-and-the-way-ahead/5329.

Other Articles, Speeches, Resources

Douglass, James.  “The Converging Martyrdom of Malcolm and Martin.” Princeton Theological Seminary, March 29, 2006. http://ratical.org/ratville/JFK/Unspeakable/ConvMartyrdom.html

King Family Press Conference on the Martin Luther King assassination conspiracy trial – see http://www.ratical.org/ratville/JFK/MLKACT/PressConference.html.

Salandria, Vincent.  “The JFK Assassination:  A False Mystery Concealing State Crimes.”  Coalition on Political Assassinations conference.  November 20, 1998, Dallas, Texas.  http://www.ratical.org/ratville/JFK/Unspeakable/COPA1998VJS.html

Schotz, E. Martin.  “The Waters of Knowledge versus The Waters of Uncertainty:  Mass Denial in the Assassination of President Kennedy,” Malignant Nature of Pseudo-Debate section.  Coalition on Political Assassinations Conference. November 20, 1998. Dallas, Texas. http://ratical.org/ratville/JFK/COPA1998EMS.html

“Trial Transcript” – Transcripts of the Martin Luther King’s assassination conspiracy trial – see http://ratical.org/ratville/JFK/MLKACT/.  The legal designation for the trial is:

Plaintiffs,

Defendants.
